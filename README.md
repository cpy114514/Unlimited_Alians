## Unlimited Alians
This is a party game that allows maxium 6 players to play. 
Player can have a relax or a tension game with their friends, family, or anyone else.
You are tring to reach the end and don't let anyone else reach it!
# Party mode:
player alowed to build the stuffs they get in party box. The first player reach 6 points will win the game
# Beetle:
an basic enemy that you can step on it
# Massive Beetle:
a bigger beetle
# Beetle with spike on it:
you should not step on it
# Black hole:
"Suck" everything in
# Coin:
give you 0.5 extra points
# Diamond:
give you 1 extra point
# Fireball Launcher:
it shoots fireball
# Bat:
a flighing enemy
# Ladder
something to climb
# Rotating saw
watch out! it's dangerous
# Portal
faster way to travel
# Trampoline
jump higher
# Tag mode:
player have to watch out to not get blome up by the bomb and try to give the bomb away to other players. The players final survived will win.
